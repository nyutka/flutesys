Still Have Questions?

How does the money back guarantee work?

You can cancel anytime, by clicking a button on the dashboard. Then, if you ask for your money back, we will refund your last monthly bill. If you signed up for an annual plan, we will refund the entire amount prorated to the end of the previous month. The money back guarantee DOES NOT apply to the Pay As You Go plan.

Do you offer a free trial? Is a credit card required?

We do NOT offer free trials, but we offer money back guarantees instead. Or you can try our Zero Monthly plan! And yes, we require a credit card for verification purposes: to prevent spammers from using our email service.

What is a failover?

A failover happens when we detect an email API error or delivery failure with your primary provider. We attempt to automatically fix that by sending through our specially configured infrastructure. Sometimes we fix it successfully, and sometimes the error is unfixable (such as certain kinds of hard bounces).

For our Pay As You Go Plan, we only charge for successful failovers â€” those failovers which we managed to deliver properly without any more errors.

What if I don't use my full monthly quota?

Your credits roll over! Any unused email quota on your account roll over to the next month. And those credits keep rolling, forever.

What if I go over the monthly quota limit?

We will send you email warnings before you reach your quota, so you can upgrade your account before you hit the limit. If you actually go over the limit, emails will fail and return an error from our API.

Do I still need an account with SendGrid/Mailgun/etc?

Yes, we are not an email provider, we are only a failover service. You stil need to have an account with one of our supported primary providers.
